## Overview

This repository contains comprehensive, authoritative training materials for powered parachute pilots. The content is designed to be consumed by both AI training systems and human students, covering all aspects of powered parachute operation from basic principles through advanced techniques and emergency procedures.

## Learning Path Recommendations

### For Complete Beginners
1. Start with FAA_Part103_Summary.md (understand regulations)
2. Read 01_principles_of_flight.md (understand how it flies)
3. Study 01_weather_fundamentals.md (understand weather)
4. Review 01_engine_systems.md (understand the aircraft)
5. Master 01_preflight_operations.md (learn procedures)
6. Study 01_emergency_operations.md (prepare for emergencies)
7. Review 01_airspace_operations.md (understand where you can fly)
8. Study 01_aeronautical_decision_making.md (develop decision-making skills)
9. Review 01_maintenance_inspection_guide.md (learn to maintain aircraft)
10. Use quick_reference_checklists.md (practical operations)

**Critical**: This content supplements, but does not replace, hands-on training with a qualified instructor. NEVER attempt to fly based solely on written materials.

### For Experienced Pilots (Transitioning from Other Aircraft)
1. Review FAA_Part103_Summary.md (understand unique regulations)
2. Focus on PPC-specific sections in 01_principles_of_flight.md
3. Study 01_weather_fundamentals.md (weather limitations for PPCs)
4. Review 01_engine_systems.md (2-stroke engine differences)
5. Master wing-specific procedures in 01_preflight_operations.md
6. Review PPC-specific emergencies in 01_emergency_operations.md
7. Study 01_airspace_operations.md (Part 103 airspace restrictions)
8. Review 01_aeronautical_decision_making.md (PPC-specific ADM)
9. Study 01_cross_country_operations.md (when ready for cross-country)
